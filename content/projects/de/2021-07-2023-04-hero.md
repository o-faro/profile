## Context

Implementation and further development of the Mazda UK online shop.

## Role & setup

As a freelancing frontend developer for Dept®.

## Problem space

The Mazda UK online shop is the central platform for selling vehicles, accessories, and services online.  
It integrates various backend systems and requires a consistent user experience across devices.

## Key decisions & solutions

- Development of reusable UI components with React and TypeScript
- Server-side rendering and routing using Next.js
- State management with Redux and Redux-Thunk
- Component-driven development and documentation with Storybook
- Styling with SCSS

## Quality & collaboration

- Unit and integration tests with Jest and React Testing Library
- Collaboration within an agile, cross-functional team
